Tom and Jerry

Sleepy-Time Tom (1951) Would Rather Have A Nap Than A Home

Tom spends the entire night performing raucous songs with Meathead, Lightning, and Butch, only returning home at sunrise. Mammy orders him to safeguard the fridge from Jerry even though the cat can barely keep his eyes open. Jerry uses the opportunity to mess with his nemesis by creating a sleep-friendly environment for Tom.

By the end of the episode, Mammy catches Tom fast asleep on her bed and instantly kicks him out as promised. Tom doesn't even care anymore at this point, and continues sleeping until his feline pals return for another night of caterwauling.
